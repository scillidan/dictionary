What is "landmarks" in epub?

```md
In the context of EPUB (Electronic Publication) files, which are a standard format for e-books, "landmarks" refer to a set of elements within the EPUB that provide key navigation points for readers. These landmarks are part of the EPUB's internal structure and help define important sections or points in the book, such as:

1. **Cover Page**: Indicates the cover image or cover section of the book.
2. **Table of Contents**: A navigational aid listing chapters and sections.
3. **Start of Book**: The beginning of the main content, where the reader should ideally start reading.
4. **Chapters and Sections**: Key parts of the book that are important for navigation.
5. **Appendix**: Additional material or supplementary information at the end of the book.
6. **Index**: A list of topics or names at the end of the book for reference.

Landmarks help improve accessibility and usability, allowing reading systems, such as e-reader apps, to provide enhanced navigation features for moving between critical parts of the book. They can be especially useful in long documents or educational texts, making it easier for readers to locate important sections without having to scroll through the entire content. In EPUB 3, these landmarks are defined using semantic markup within the publication's package document, often in the form of metadata and navigational files.
```

在 EPUB（电子出版物）文件的上下文中，EPUB 是电子书的标准格式，“地标”指的是 EPUB 中的一组元素，这些元素为读者提供了关键的导航点。这些地标是 EPUB 内部结构的一部分，有助于定义书中的重要章节或节点，例如：

1. **封面页**：指示书籍的封面图片或封面部分。
2. **目录**：一个导航辅助，列出章节和部分。
3. **图书开始部分**：主要内容的起点，理想情况下是读者开始阅读的位置。
4. **章节和部分**：书中对导航重要的关键部分。
5. **附录**：书末的附加材料或补充信息。
6. **索引**：书末的主题或名称列表，供参考使用。

地标有助于提升可访问性和可用性，使阅读系统（如电子阅读器应用）能够提供增强的导航功能，方便在书的关键部分之间切换。它们在长篇文档或教育文本中特别有用，使读者无需滚动整个内容即可轻松定位重要章节。在 EPUB 3 中，这些地标通过在出版物的包文档内使用语义标记来定义，通常以元数据和导航文件的形式存在。
